About Us

Velocity Sneakers is a sneaker manufracturer selling the most trendy, comfortable and affordable sneakers.

Our founders

Velocity Sneakers is promoted by Nikhil Ven and Andrew Draw. They graduated from IIT Delhi in 1992, specialising in textile and leather engineering. The first factory was set up in Kanpur while the first showroom was set up in New Delhi. The company has grown from strength to strength and now has 30 retail showrooms in NCR and 3 factories in Agra, Lucknow and Noida.

Our products

Velocity Sneakers is a reputed sneaker manufacturer which has 30 outlets across Delhi. We sell sneakers online pan India. Vendraw Sneakers provides casual, trendy, comfortable, sneakers for targetting the youth at an affordable price point. The products are available for men, women and kids.
